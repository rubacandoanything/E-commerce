Brigitte Midi dress| Soiree Floral

Our Brigitte Midi Dress is designed to be identifiable yet elusive. With a flattering shirred silhouette, this piece features a voluminous mid arm sleeve and an over-the-knee length with bottom frill detail. Our exclusive Soiree Floral, inspired by watercolour floral art, is eco printed with European non-toxic Oeko Tex Certified Eco Passport inks.

Violette Midi Dress | Meadow Blush

Our Violette midi dress is the spring/summer midi dress of your dreams. Featuring the ability to wear on or off the shoulder, the Violette features an elasticised waist and neckline, bow tie sleeves and a midi cut. Featured here in our exclusive Meadow Blush print.

Alexa Maxi Wrap Dress | Magnolia

ur Alexa Maxi Wrap Dress features capped sleeves with pin tuck detail, waist wrap style, and subtle gather features. Our exclusive Magnolia print is eco printed with European non-toxic Oeko Tex Certified Eco Passport inks on soft 100% Ecovero Viscose fabric.
